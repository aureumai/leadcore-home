Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about LeadCore.

How does the Gmail integration work?
Our Gmail integration uses OAuth to securely access your emails. Once connected, LeadCore can analyze your conversations to identify potential leads, automatically extract contact information, and organize it in your dashboard. Your email data remains private and secure throughout this process.
Is my data safe with LeadCore?
Absolutely. LeadCore is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with end-to-end encryption. We follow industry best practices for security, use OAuth for third-party integrations, and never store your credentials. Your data privacy is our top priority.
How long does it take to set up LeadCore?
Most customers are fully set up and running within 1-2 hours. The initial account creation takes just minutes, and our integration wizards guide you through connecting your Gmail and JobNimbus accounts. If you need assistance, our support team is available to help.
Can I export my data from LeadCore?
Yes, you can export your lead data at any time in CSV or Excel format. This ensures you always have access to your information and can use it in other systems if needed.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start your trial, and you can upgrade to a paid plan at any time.
